Varuna Brull

Content Management/Wix

Varuna is a freelance photographer based in Edinburgh.

She had a website showcasing her portfolio but she felt the design needed some improvement and she wanted to include a blog and a contact section and being able to upload new content and update the blog by herself.

A minimalists design with clear background was chosen to maximize the impact of Varuna’s work. The home page shows two images part of the site’s collection and presents the user with a simple navigation bar & a link to Instagram.

The footer is very simple with just the copyright and web design credits.

Maite Granados

HTML CSS/SASS

Maite is a Spanish artist based in Mallorca.

She was looking to have her first website showing drawings and prints that form part or her work, and also to having links to social media and her Etsy shop. She preferred a simple and functional design.

The website was created in Spanish, with a fixed side bar was created to leave the rest of the page as a gallery.

The colour blue of the site reflects on the predominant presence of the sea as a main topic in Maite's work; and the navigation is divided in the different sections most representative of her collection.

Maite's Website

The Orchard Bar

HTML CSS/SASS JAVASCRIPT

The Orchard is a Bar an Restaurant situated near the Royal Botanic Garden in Edinburgh.

They asked me to update the design of their website and to include a booking system for their busy restaurant service, up to date menus and opening hours.

Later of Covid information became a vital part of the information offered in the website and the constant changes in opening hours and regulations made challenging the website maintenance.
To solve this JavaScript became very useful allowing for multiple changes through the website, updating only the JS file.

The Alchemist's Cave

HTML CSS


The Alchemist's cave is a tattoo and body art studio in Easter Rd, Edinburgh.

Their website needed an update to include a gallery, a contact from and social media integration. They already had a logo and they wanted to make this a central part of the design.

A simple layout with black background and a Cinzel font matched their expectations and the website serves them now to receive bookings and enquiries through their email account and showcase their work, social media and general information.

Victor Blanco

HTML CSS/SASS & E-commerce


This was the first website I ever made and it serves me as a way of showing my side hobby; relief printing. It also works as a shop where I can sell my prints.

It is a simple website that displays information about the my prints and works as a font for a small shop hosted in Big Cartel.
This allows users to buy using credit cards and PayPal and it calculates postage costs.

The website uses Poppins font and a clear blue background to keep the centre of attention in the prints.
The website also includes a bio page.

Victor's Website
